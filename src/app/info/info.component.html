How it Works!

The classes in highschool fall into one of three categories: Academic, Honors, Advanced Placement. Your academic classes are out of a 4.0 scale, Honors classes out of a 4.5 scale and Advanced Placement Classes out of a 5.0 scale. Each year your final grade is used to calculate your GPA. Your yearly GPA is averaged and then sent to colleges.

Why is your GPA important?

Colleges use your GPA as a way to measure your academic excellence. They want to see that you are taking challenging courses as well as getting good grades.
